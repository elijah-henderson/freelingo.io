The open source and community-powered language learning app.

Learn the most common words.


We sought to create an app that teaches the most important words, not useless phrases. We will take you straight to the words to learn the language directly. And we will explain the grammar, pronunciation, and syntax for each language offered by Freelingo.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or if you are interested in contributing to freelingo.io, please email us. Be sure to let us know if you experience any bugs or unexpected errors while using our app.
